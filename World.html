World

The limits of my language mean the limits of my world.

Languages represent the world we live in. French speakers use the same word (Papillon) to describe moths and butterflies. A world that makes the difference between a moth and a butterfly and a world that doesn't would be very different.The application World is made for expanding our world.

  • Timeline: 3 months

  • Client: Personal Project

  • Tools: Figma, Photoshop

  • Role: Research, Wireframe, Design, Prototype

Context

Due to the increase in the number of Internet users and the development of social media culture, slangs or abbreviations have been overflowing in recent decades, and these linguistic phenomena can be found easily even in news and papers.

Recreating new contextual words is very much in line with the economy of language. Unfortunately, however, more and more people find it difficult to get the context out of long texts or to explain how they feel without neologisms or abbreviations due to their lack of vocabulary.

Problems

1. Excessive exposure to neologisms and acronyms
It is easy to find slangs and abbreviations on the Internet, especially social media and even in the news, as a result being overexposed to slangs or abbreviations.


2. Increased difficulty using proper language
Without any slangs or abbreviations, there is a big difficulty to express feelings/opinions.

Insight

There is a need for the service that expand the limits of the world by improving the writing skills of modern people

Desk Research

1. An increase in the number of people who find it difficult to write

As a result of Job Korea, job search website, surveying 1,267 male and female office workers on ‘Writing at work’, 97.4% of office workers answered that they want to write well and it was found that an average score of 6 out of 10 was given to their own writing skills.

2. A sharp decrease in the numberㆍopportunities to write

In a survey of 315 job seekers on Career, job search site, as to the reason why they have difficulty writing, the largest percentage of interviewee said that they don’t write much in their daily life (39%).

3. Growing demand for writing skills

According to Kyobo Books, the sales of writing skill-related books from January to September in 2021 increased by 15.6% compared to the same period last year.


Through desk research, I found that there are many people who have difficulty writing, and yet the number of times they write in their daily life is significantly lower than in the past. Nevertheless, as the importance of writing is growing, it is clear that a service to form a writing habit in the long term is necessary.



Goal

Inspire will of users to write and improve their writing skills

User Interview

Then, what makes it difficult for people to have the will to write, and what are the practical difficulties in writing good materials?
In order to derive answers to the above two points of view, user interviews were conducted with the individuals who are interested in writing.



User A

- At first, I work hard, but it is difficult to maintain the habit of writing because of the lack of motivation.

- I don't know which topic to choose or how to start an introduction.

User B

- I feel pressure to be a talented writer.

- I would like to refer to a well-written cover letter, but I am not sure which part to refer to and how.

User C

- I often write only short comments on social media so that I lose concentration when writing long articles.

- Since there is no feedback on my pieces, I don't have any clue if I’m writing well.

Pain Point

1. Having trouble with forming a habit for writing consistently
2. The technical limitations of good writing

Journey Map

After the user interview, I could see the need for a means to help form habits before and after writing and to overcome technical difficulties during writing



Solution

1. Provides a trigger to start writing easily
2. Provides a writing guideline in order to make out fine texts
3. Motivation to keep writing consistently

Output

1. Provides a trigger to start writing easily

◼ Form a personalized writing habit depends on the user

If it feels like a challenge when forming a new habit, it becomes very hard to maintain. Making the process of getting started easy is very effective in keeping up a habit. When it’s started with a slightly easier goal like 'sitting at your desk for five minutes' than 'write one post a day', it will be possible to build a solid foundation for maintaining the habit.


Step 1

Understanding my writing level and tendency


: An onboarding process for personalized recommendations, gathering information such as writing level, purpose, favoire genre, etc.

Step 2

Recommendation of habit steps which are appropriate for user


: Recommendation of writing habit steps (goals and procedures for habit formation) which are customized to user as a result of onboarding

Step 3

Create own habit steps


: If the recommended habit stage is not satisfactory, the user directly forms the habit stage in the desired way





◼ Screen composition that encourages users to write

Home screen is designed in such a way that users can naturally want to write something. In order to reduce the burden and time required for selecting a topic, the application recommend automatically the proper writing materials.

2. Provides a guideline in order to make out fine texts

◼ A system that allows users to be immersed in writing

As the writing continues, it helps users write “fine texts” rather than simple texts. It provides guidelines to maintain a constant flow of context, and enables users to write without interruption through real-time feedback.








◼ Improving writing skills through feedback

When spending time with people who have similar habit, it’s easily become permanent. As users having similar writing habits interact with each other, the habits become strongly fixed to users.



Option 1

Short feedback to other users

Option 2

Share specific impression with people

Result

Get the rewards after sharing reviews and feedback




3. Motivation to keep writing consistently

◼ Immediate evaluation after completing texts

The more users understand their current state, the more opportunities they have to reach their goals. Through the analysis of their habits from various angles, users can focus on their strengths and make up for the weaknesses. In addition, with the feeling of private record book, it encourage not to quit the habit.








◼ Analysis and compensation provided through My Page

The most difficult reason to sustain a habit is that there is no immediate reward after practicing the habit. By providing quick analysis of the text and various rewards such as habit trackers and stamps after completing the text, it makes users look forward to next writing session.




What I've Learned

Through my first project as a UI/UX designer, I exprienced the overall design process, including ideation and creation of wireframe. In addition, I learned a research method that can lead to meaningful results through desk research and user interviews, which made it easier to approach users’ problems. Previously, I focused simply on visual and aesthetic design, but after this project, I have been able to focus more on design that relieves the user's pain.
